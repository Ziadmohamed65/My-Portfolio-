### **Course Description**

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to rigging and animation in Blender, focusing primarily on character animation. Participants will learn essential principles and practical techniques to rig, animate, and export characters for use in game engines or other digital media.

Through a structured progression, the course covers both basic and advanced rigging methods, expressive character animation, and the creation of reusable animation actions such as run cycles and attack sequences. Students will also gain hands-on experience exporting their work for integration into game development workflows.

### **Key Learning Outcomes**

* Understand core principles of rigging and animation.
* Build and refine both basic and advanced character rigs.
* Create expressive, game-ready animations efficiently.
* Export and implement animations within game engines.
