### **Course Description**

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to creating cinematic cutscenes in Unity using **Cinemachine**, **Timeline**, and **Animation** tools. Designed for aspiring game developers, it covers the full process of crafting dynamic, story-driven sequences that enhance gameplay and presentation quality.

Learners will gain the skills to design and control Cinemachine camera systems, synchronize animations with gameplay events, and apply post-processing techniques to achieve a polished, professional look. The course also guides students through building a complete cinematic trailer from concept to final render, ensuring a deep understanding of Unity’s cinematic workflow.

---

### **Key Learning Outcomes**

* Master Unity’s Cinemachine and Timeline tools for cinematic control.
* Create dynamic camera movements and seamless transitions.
* Synchronize animations and gameplay to build immersive sequences.
* Design cinematic trailers with professional lighting and effects.
* Apply post-processing to enhance visual storytelling.
